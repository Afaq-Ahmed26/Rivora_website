Your Rhythm

Four phases.
Your timeline.

Every cycle is different. Yours might be 24 days or 36 days and every phase has distinct health, nutrition, and energy patterns that Rivora adapts to specifically for you.

21 – 40+ days All cycle lengths supported · PMOS aware · Irregular mode
Phase 1

Reset Week

Typically days 1–5 (varies)

Your body renews. Rest, warmth, and comfort food are what you need. Cramps, fatigue, and lower mood are natural here.

Key Nutrition
Iron-rich foods: dal, chicken, chana · Warming foods: adrak chai, khichdi · Hydration: 2.5–3L water
Comfort & Relief
Warm water bag (heating pad) for cramps · Rest · Light movement or stretching · Dark chocolate (small amount) for mood support · Halwa or kheer in moderation
Rest Comfort Iron ↑
Phase 2

Growing Energy

Typically days 6–13 (varies)

Energy and mood climb day by day. You feel curious, social, and open to new things. The best week for plans and fresh starts.

Key Nutrition
Vitamin B: egg, dahi/yogurt, brown rice · Zinc: Pumkin seeds, green peas/matar · Lean protein: chicken , karahi, daal · Energy: whole wheat roti
Adventurous Social B Vitamins ↑
Phase 3

Peak Energy

Typically days 14–16 (varies)

Your highest confidence and natural charisma. You feel magnetic and alive. Make this week count your body is performing at its best.

Key Nutrition
Antioxidants: anar/Pomegranate, guava, spinach/palak · Light meals: dahi raita, grilled chicken · Stay well hydrated: lassi (no sugar) or nimbu pani
Confident Radiant Antioxidants ↑
Phase 4

Reflection Week

Typically days 17 onward (varies by your cycle)

Energy turns inward. You may feel more sensitive, emotional, or irritable — this is your luteal phase, and it's completely normal.

Key Nutrition
Magnesium: badam, akhrot, methi · Complex carbs: dalia, brown rice, moong daal · Mood support: haldi doodh · Reduce chai & coffee
Introspective Sensitive Magnesium ↑
